It Started With a Garden and a Lot of Time on My Hands

There was no rock bottom moment. No dramatic turning point. I was unemployed, had a little savings, and a lot of time. So I planted a garden. That's it. Nothing fancy, nothing planned on a whiteboard. Just soil, seed, and something in me that needed to grow something real. One garden became a few crop rows. A few crop rows became a farm. Heart of Texas Organics is what happens when you stop waiting for the perfect moment and just start.
