Our Ingredients & Practices

We are not USDA Certified Organic, and that is intentional. Organic certification is a process and a label. Our name reflects our standard, not a certification. We commit to no synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers, no GMOs, no added hormones or antibiotics, and no preservatives. We source locally from growers we trust and are fully transparent about every ingredient.
Yes. Our pasture-raised hens are fed a soy-free diet. They also forage freely on open pasture, which contributes to naturally richer, more nutritious yolks.
We use unbleached, unbromated flour and source from local Texas mills whenever possible. No dough conditioners, no additives. Just flour, water, salt, and time.
