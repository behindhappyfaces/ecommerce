Our Animal Philosophy

Happy animals make better food.

"Behind Happy Faces" isn't just a name — it's a standard. Every animal on this farm lives a life it was designed for: open pasture, fresh air, natural forage, and room to move. We believe the quality of what's on your plate is inseparable from the quality of life that came before it.

Our chickens are rotated daily across fresh pasture. They scratch, forage, dust-bathe, and live like chickens. Not like a commodity. When we say pasture-raised, we mean it — not a marketing term, not a loophole. Open sky, every day.

We don't raise animals. We steward them. The land is better for it, and so is the food.

Our land practices mirror our animal practices: no synthetic fertilizers, no chemical inputs. The animals are part of the ecosystem — they fertilize, they aerate, they build soil. It's a cycle that's been working for thousands of years. We're just not breaking it.

No antibiotics, ever. Healthy animals raised in healthy conditions don't need them. If an animal gets sick, it's treated and removed from the food system.

No added hormones. Growth should happen at the pace nature intended. Every time.

Rotational grazing. Our pastures rest and regenerate. The animals move. The land heals. That's the whole system.

On-farm processing. We handle harvest ourselves, with care and transparency. You know exactly where your food comes from.
